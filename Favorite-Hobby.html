Perennials

Perennials are plants that usually live for more than 2 years and usually return seasonally. Annuals are plants that are short lived and only live for one season. Colorado has a wide variety of beautiful Perennials that return each year. When planning or looking to add plants to the garden I turn to perennials.

Gardening is very calming and a great stress releaver. Definitly is theraputic is nature. There is nothing better than getting your hands dirty in soil and the smell of the earth. One does not need to be an expert gardener in order to create lovely garden scapes, preferably with Perennials.
